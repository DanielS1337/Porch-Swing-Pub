About the Porch Swing Pub

Helping neighbors become friends, friends become lovers, and lovers become enemies since good ol' 2008. If you are a stranger to the folks in this bar then just give it a few minutes, we promise that whether you like it or not you are going to make friends quickly.

The Swing is a great place to host a happy hour party, watch a game, or just sit out on the patio and relax. It doesn't matter if you are wearing a tie or wearing flip flops, if you come from work or from the pool, you're always welcome here. Bring your boyfriend, girlfriend, wife, wife's girlfriend, wife's girlfriend’s sister, whatever floats your boat. We're all here to have a good time RIGHT?

HD TV's blanket the bar both inside and out, with all the sports packages that any self respecting sports fanatic could appreciate. Sorry no basket weaving channel. We have 2 dart boards, a pool table, and a Golden Tee, so if you want some good sports activity but are too tired to run around Memorial Park then you can stop here and get a good all around workout

We have 38 beers on draft, another 30 or 40 in a bottle, enough distilled spirits to kill a couple horses and even a little bit of wine for sipping. Our food menu has something for everyone, from fried pickles to bacon and jalapeno wrapped chicken. Whether it's lunch you're looking for or a late night snack at 1 am we have something to fill your hole.

So next time you're in the neighborhood stop by this neighborhood bar and come make some new friends.
